Our Story

Born from a passion to share the darkest skies with the world.

From Backyard to Remote Observatory

It started with a small refractor under city lights. I knew that to truly capture the cosmos, I had to escape the light pollution. After months of planning and building, our Bortle 1 observatory was born – now open to you.

We believe astrophotography should be accessible to everyone, no matter where you live.
